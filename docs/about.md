# About Me

I am a remote sensing scientist and lecturer at **Wollo University**, Ethiopia. My research bridges satellite Earth observation and machine learning to address environmental challenges in the Horn of Africa.

## Research interests

- **Land use / land cover change** — multi-temporal analysis using Landsat and Sentinel-2
- **Machine learning for geospatial data** — deep learning on satellite imagery
- **Agricultural monitoring** — crop type mapping, yield estimation, water use efficiency
- **Drought and climate** — drought assessment, climate variability impacts
- **Environmental change** — deforestation, desertification, wetland dynamics, aerosol monitoring
