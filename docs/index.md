## Short biography

I am a researcher and educator specializing in **satellite remote sensing**, **machine learning**, and **geospatial data analysis** at Wollo University, Ethiopia. My work bridges Earth observation and artificial intelligence to address environmental challenges in the Horn of Africa — including land cover change, agricultural monitoring, drought assessment, and climate impacts.
