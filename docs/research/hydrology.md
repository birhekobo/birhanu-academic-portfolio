# Hydrology

## Overview

Applying remote sensing and GIS to hydrological modeling and surface water dynamics monitoring in Ethiopian watersheds.

## Key areas

- **Surface water extent** — Landsat and Sentinel-1 based water mapping
- **Watershed modeling** — SWAT, HEC-HMS integrated with EO data
- **Soil moisture** — SMAP, Sentinel-1 soil moisture retrieval
- **Flood mapping** — SAR-based flood extent and damage assessment

## Methods

```{list-table}
:header-rows: 1

* - Application
  - Approach
  - Data
* - Water body mapping
  - NDWI, MNDWI, Otsu thresholding
  - Landsat, Sentinel-2
* - Flood mapping
  - SAR backscatter thresholding
  - Sentinel-1
* - Soil moisture
  - Change detection / neural networks
  - Sentinel-1, SMAP
* - Hydrological modeling
  - SWAT model calibration
  - GEE, GIS data
```

## Applications

- Lake Tana basin hydrology
- Flood risk assessment in lowland areas
- Irrigation water accounting
- Wetland conservation planning
