# Remote Sensing

## Overview

My research in remote sensing focuses on the analysis of satellite imagery for environmental monitoring in East Africa. I work with optical (Landsat, Sentinel-2, MODIS) and SAR (Sentinel-1) data to map land surface properties and their changes over time.

## Key areas

- **Land use/land cover classification** — supervised and unsupervised methods
- **Change detection** — multi-temporal analysis for deforestation, urban expansion
- **Vegetation monitoring** — NDVI, EVI, and other spectral indices time series
- **SAR interferometry** — surface deformation and wetland mapping

## Applications

- Forest cover change in the Lake Tana basin
- Agricultural land mapping in the Rift Valley
- Wetland dynamics in the Ethiopian highlands
- Urban expansion around Dessie city
