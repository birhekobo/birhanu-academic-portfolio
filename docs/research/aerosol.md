# Aerosol

## Overview

Monitoring atmospheric aerosol concentrations over East Africa using satellite remote sensing to understand air quality, dust transport, and aerosol-climate interactions.

## Data sources

- **MODIS** — AOD (Aerosol Optical Depth) at 550 nm
- **VIIRS** — AOD and aerosol type
- **Sentinel-5P TROPOMI** — Aerosol index, NO₂, SO₂
- **CALIPSO** — Vertical aerosol profiles
- **CAMS reanalysis** — Modeled aerosol fields

## Key research questions

- What are the spatiotemporal patterns of AOD over the Horn of Africa?
- How does dust transport from the Sahara and Arabian Peninsula affect regional air quality?
- What are the impacts of biomass burning aerosols on regional climate?

## Methods

- Satellite AOD retrieval validation against AERONET
- Time series trend analysis
- Source apportionment using trajectory models (HYSPLIT)
- Radiative forcing estimation
