# Climate Change

## Overview

Investigating the impacts of climate variability on land surface processes and ecosystem dynamics in the Horn of Africa using satellite Earth observation and reanalysis data.

## Key focus areas

- Long-term trends in vegetation greenness and productivity
- Land-atmosphere interactions under changing climate
- Climate extremes and their impact on agriculture
- Land surface temperature trends

## Data sources

- MODIS LST and vegetation products (MOD11, MOD13)
- CHIRPS precipitation data
- ERA5 reanalysis
- CMIP6 climate projections

## Methods

- Trend analysis (Mann-Kendall, Sen's slope)
- Breakpoint detection in time series
- Correlation and regression analysis
- Machine learning for climate downscaling
