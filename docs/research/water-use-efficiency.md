# Water Use Efficiency

## Overview

Assessing agricultural water productivity in Ethiopian farming systems using remote sensing-based evapotranspiration and biomass estimation.

## Approach

- **Actual evapotranspiration (ETa)** — SEBAL, METRIC, SSEBop models
- **Biomass production** — light use efficiency models (CASA, MOD17)
- **Water productivity** — kg of yield per m³ of water consumed

## Methods

| Component | Method | Data |
|-----------|--------|------|
| ETa | SEBAL / METRIC | Landsat, Sentinel-2 |
| Biomass | CASA model | MODIS, Landsat |
| Yield | Empirical models | Field survey |
| Water productivity | WP = Yield / ETa | Integrated |

## Applications

- Benchmarking water productivity in the Rift Valley
- Identifying water-saving opportunities in irrigated agriculture
- Assessing climate resilience of different cropping systems
